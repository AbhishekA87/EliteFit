Overview

EliteFit is a premium fitness network designed for upscale residential societies. Our goal is to create a consistent, branded gym experience inside residential buildings—similar to Airbnb/OYO’s standardization model.

Membership Models

Monthly Subscription

Flat monthly rate for unlimited access to the society gym and group sessions.

Annual Upfront Plan

One-time payment with long-term benefits, VIP features, and fitness goodies.

Pay Per Session

No strings attached. Residents can pay for each class or PT session as they go.

Session Types

Group Classes

From yoga to HIIT, we offer diverse weekly classes for the community.

Personal Training

1-on-1 attention for residents who want personalized workout plans.

Key Benefits

For Residents

Access to a premium gym just steps away, flexible plans, and exclusive sessions.

For Societies

Increased property value, shared revenue model, branded fitness reputation.

For Investors

Scalable, asset-light operations, recurring revenue, and high retention potential.
